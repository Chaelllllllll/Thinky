About Thinky

Thinky is a study platform where learners can discover, publish, and practice with educational reviewers, quizzes, and flashcards.

What We Do

We help students and reviewers organize learning materials in one place so studying is easier, clearer, and more collaborative.

  • Public and private reviewer collections
  • Built-in quizzes and flashcards for active recall
  • Community features with moderation controls

Our Principles

  • Educational value first
  • Clear community standards
  • Respect for user privacy and safety
  • Continuous platform improvement

For moderation rules, visit our Community Policies.

Who This Site Is For

Thinky is designed for students, tutors, and educators who want practical tools to prepare for classes and exams.

To start using the platform, you can create an account or browse public content.
